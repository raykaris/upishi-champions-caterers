About Us

Upishi Champions Caterers (UCC) have the ability to be completely mobile to participate in wide array of remote evevnts from corporate outings to formal weddings, and emergency relief to carnival-themed birthday parties. Very few catering companies offer as much diversity with professional event planners to assist with the management from start to finish.

Our Services

Wedding & pre-wedding

We offer exceptional wedding and pre-wedding services designed to make your special moments unforgatable.
From elegant multi-course meals to beautifully curated buffets, we tailor every detail to match your vision.
Our team ensures a seamless dining experience with top-notch service, exquisite flavors, and stunning presentation.

Carnival

Our carnival catering service brings the excitement and vibrant flavors of a classic fair right to your event.
We offer a wide array of fun and delicious treats.
Whether you're hosting a corporate event, school festival, or private party, our team ensures a festive atmosphere filled with colorful stalls and mouth-watering snacks.
Let us turn your celebration into a joyful carnival experience for guests of all age.

Corporate Events

Our corporate event catering service is designed to elevate your business gatherings with impeccable dining experiences.
We provide tailored menus that suit and corporate occasion and your brand's image.
From gourmet breakfast spreads and elegant lunches to cocktail receptions and themed dinners, we handle every detail to ensure a seamless event.

Rental Equipment

We offer a wide range of high-quality tools and essentials to make your event a seamless success.
From elegant tableware, chairs, and tables to sophisticated equipment such as beverage dispensers, chafing dishes, and cooking stations, we have everything you need.
Our well maintained inventory ensures style and functionality.
Count on us for timely delivery, setup assistance, and flexible rental options tailored to fit your event's unique requirements.
